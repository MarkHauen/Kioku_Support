🛡️ Security & Privacy

Your health data is deeply personal. That's why Kioku uses industry-standard encryption to protect your information:

✓ Local Encryption — All medication and habit data encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM
✓ No Accounts — No login required, no cloud syncing to servers (unless you choose to use your own backend)
✓ No Tracking — No analytics, no ads, no third-party SDKs
✓ Device Control — Keys stored in the Keychain, protected by your device

🤔 FAQ

Will Kioku work offline?

Yes. Kioku works entirely offline. Reminders, medication logging, and habit tracking all work without an internet connection.

What about syncing to my iPhone and Apple Watch?

iPhone and Apple Watch sync automatically using end-to-end encryption. Your data never touches the internet unless you explicitly set up your own personal backend.

Does Kioku sell my data?

Absolutely not. We don't collect it, don't share it, and don't have accounts or logins. Your data belongs to you.

Is Kioku free?

Yes, Kioku is available for free on the Apple App Store. No subscriptions, no ads, no hidden fees.
